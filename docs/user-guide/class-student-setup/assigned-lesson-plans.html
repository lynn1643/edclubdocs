Lesson plans available on TypingClub

The various lesson plans offered by TypingClub can be found:
Lesson Plans Lesson Plan Library.

The library contains our flagship lesson plan Typing Jungle, a fun-filled curriculum for kindergarten students called Jungle Junior, a short typing course Typing Basics, a lesson plan for typing Dvorak, lesson plans for one-handed typists, lesson plans to learn to type in multiple foreign languages, and stories from our Animated Story Typing series called Ava and The Rabbit, Going Solo, and The Perfect Match. Any of these lesson plans can be installed in your account at no additional cost.


What is my default lesson plan?

The default lesson plan for every class and account is Typing Jungle. The default lesson plan is the activity that will be automatically assigned to all classes in your account. If you prefer to use a different lesson plan as the default in your account, remember to make the change before you upload your classes. To view or update your default lesson plan, go to:
Lesson Plans Default Lesson Plan Settings.

Notice: Note that you have the ability to assign a different lesson plan to each grade level.

How do I update assignements after I created my classes?

If you already created your classes, you can still change the lesson plans assigned:

  • Update class assignments individually:
    1. Go to the Classes tab.
    2. Click open the class of interest.
    3. Click Lesson Plans and assign/unassign lesson plans.

  • Update class assignments in bulk:
    1. Go to the Classes tab.
    2. Select all or multiple classes by checking the box in front of them.
    3. Click Bulk Update
    4. Assing or remove lesson plan.
