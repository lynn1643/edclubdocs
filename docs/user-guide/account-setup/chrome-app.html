Chrome app

To streamline the sign-in process, you can install the EdClub Chrome app. After a one-time setup process, the app will direct your students to the correct portal URL.

Notice: The EdClub app is not searchable publicly from the Google App Store. To install it, you must click the following link: EdClub app

This will guide your students through the process of finding your school's portal URL and show them how to use it to sign into EdClub.
